这是我在学习小提琴的过程中记录的一些练习和课程内容。
本章记录王一潼老师在2025年7月27日的课程内容。

练习注意点

音阶练习:卡尔弗来什D大调音阶 b小调音阶

  1. 使用全弓。弓根在平时很少用到，因此音阶练习时要注意使用弓根。
  2. 放松双肩，注意运弓子轨迹，下半弓调动大臂，上半弓调动小臂，手腕只有在弓根处有轻微的动作。可以靠墙练习。
  3. 换弦时减弓速加弓压，提前准备好换弦动作，减少杂音。
  4. 着重练习换把，一指通过和大拇指一同滑动顶开二指到位。要有提前量。

练习曲: 克莱采尔第2首

  1. 在中弓练，每个音顿一次，还有音跟音连在一起两种练法，注意音准。
  2. 60拍每分钟的速度练习。

布置：第三号小步舞曲（巴赫）

Foreword

This documents my practice notes and lesson content during violin studies.
This chapter records the lesson taught by Teacher Wang Yitong on July 27, 2025.

Practice Notes

Scale Practice: Flesch D Major Scale & B Minor Scale

  1. Use full bow. Since the frog is rarely utilized in daily practice, pay special attention to using it during scale exercises.
  2. Relax both shoulders. Focus on bow trajectory: engage upper arm for lower half, forearm for upper half, with minimal wrist movement only near the frog. Wall practice recommended.
  3. When changing strings: reduce bow speed while increasing pressure. Prepare the string change in advance to minimize noise.
  4. Emphasize shifting practice - first finger slides with thumb to position second finger. Maintain anticipation.

Etude: Kreutzer No. 2

  1. Practice at middle bow using two approaches:
    • Detached notes (martelé)
    • Legato (connected notes)
      Maintain precise intonation.
  2. Practice at 60 BPM tempo.

Assignment: Minuet No. 3 (Bach)
